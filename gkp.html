Gorakhpur city

  1. Gorakhpur is a city located in the eastern part of the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.
  2. It is situated near the border of Nepal and is known for its cultural and historical significance.
  3. The city is named after Gorakhnath, a famous saint and founder of the Nath monastic movement.
  4. Gorakhpur is also famous for the Gorakhnath Temple, which is a major pilgrimage site for Hindus.
  5. The city has a rich history, with evidence of human settlements dating back to ancient times.
  6. Gorakhpur has a vibrant economy, with industries like agriculture, textiles, and handicrafts contributing to its growth.
