Agra city

1.Agra Fort is a historical fort in the city of Agra.
2.It was the primary residence of emperors of the Mughal Dynasty till 1638.
3.Some 4,000 builders worked on it daily for eight years, completing it in 1573.
4.The fort contains splendid palaces both in red sandstone and white marble.
5.Akbar built the fort.
6.When viewed from above, the fort displays the shape of a semicircle.
7. The walls of the fort are 2 km in perimeter and 70 feet in height.
8. The primary attraction in the fort is Jahangir's Palace (Akbar's son).
9.After the death of Shah Jahan (Grandson of Akbar), the fort was seized and plundered by many emperors.
