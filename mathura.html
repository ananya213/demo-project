Mathura city

  • Mathura is a holy city in Uttar Pradesh, Northern India.
  • It is famous as the birthplace of the lord krishna and is on the banks of the Yamuna river.
  • Mathura attracts thousands of Hindu devotees the year round all the holy places in the region.
  • The many sites and temples dedicated to Krishna are usually the major spots of attraction. Most of the pilgrims come to Mathura during Janmashthami which is considered to be the birth date of Krishna.
  • Mathura is quite well connected with the rest of the country.
